The ItQan Story

2020 - The Idea

Founded by a team of educators and tech enthusiasts who recognized the need for more personalized and effective online assessment tools. The pandemic highlighted the gap in traditional online learning methods.

2022 - The Launch

After two years of development and testing, ItQan launched with its first AI-powered adaptive assessment system. We started with 100 beta users who helped us refine the platform.

2024 - Global Growth

Today, ItQan serves over 50,000 students across 150+ countries, with partnerships with leading educational institutions and a 98% user satisfaction rate.
